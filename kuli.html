Kuli Bokiba Recipe


INGREDIENTS

  1. 3 cups smoked tuna or 3 cans tuna chunks
  2. 1 cup coconut
  3. 1½ cups rice
  4. 2 Dorset Naga
  5. ⅓ cup curry leaves
  6. 1 cup onion
  7. 5 cloves garlic
  8. 1 tbsp ginger
  9. ½ cup lemon juice
  10. ½ tsp turmeric powder
  11. 1½ cups water

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Squash the onions, garlic, ginger, curry leaves, chilies, lemon juice, turmeric powder and salt
  2. Add the tuna, rice, coconut and finally the water and mix into a thick paste-like mixture
  3. Spread evenly on a non-stick pan and bake at moderate (gas mark 4 / 180˚C / 350 ˚F) heat, until it turns brown (about 45 minutes
