Mashuni Recipe


INGREDIENTS

  1. 1 cup coconut
  2. 1 cup smoked tuna or 1 can tuna chunks
  3. 1 scotch bonnet
  4. ½ cup onion
  5. ½ cup lemon juice

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Squash the onions, and scotch bonnet with the lemon juice and salt
  2. Add the tuna and mix well
  3. Mix in the coconut
  4. Serve with roshi or farata
